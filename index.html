PRODUCT

Intelligent Adaptive Learning Platform
Our Platform which hosts Artificial Intelligence algorithms, mimics the qualities of a 'Great Teacher' that bridges the gap between Student and the Subject. We apply Artificial Intelligence and Graph Theory to the different components of the learning. By collecting data points from our platform, AI helps us to identify the gaps in the learning of the student and creates a personalised learning plan to master the subject.

ABOUT

Research suggests that there is a non-uniformity in the distribution of quality teacher. Owing to the large Student to Teacher Ratio in conventional classrooms, it is very difficult to provide personalised learning path for every student, as every student is unique in terms of learning capabilities.

Founded in 2021 by the graduates of IIT and NIT, Eigen Learning solves the fundamental problem of bridging the gap between the number of quality teachers and students.

Our Platform acts as an enabling tool for Teachers and Educational Institutions for providing personalised one-to-one tutoring using AI + Human Teacher Model.
